# Story Premise

Every great game starts with a compelling story hook.

## The Logline

If you had to pitch your story in ONE sentence, what would it be?

A good logline includes:
- The world/setting
- The protagonist
- The conflict/goal
- The stakes

## Emotional Hook

What feeling should players have throughout the experience?
