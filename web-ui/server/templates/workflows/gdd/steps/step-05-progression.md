## Types of Progression

Common types:
- **Character/Stats**: Get stronger numbers
- **Abilities/Skills**: Unlock new verbs
- **Story**: Narrative reveals
- **Meta**: Persistent unlocks across runs
- **Mastery**: Player skill improvement
